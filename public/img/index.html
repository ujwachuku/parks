Who we are

We are pleased to introduce FunPlex, an amusement park and recreational centre, a subsidiary of Allied Thrust & Systems (Nigeria) Limited (ATS). We are a wholly indigenous company incorporated in 1992 and we provide Entertainment and Hospitality Management Services.

Quality Driven Service

Our wealth of experience in entertainment and hospitality has not only given us an insight to what people want but also made us an outstanding organization known for Quality Driven Services. We promise professionalism, excellent service delivery, ethical practices, quality standards and many more.

Fun Space

FunPlex is laid out over 10000 sq meters, providing a breathtaking atmosphere, safety and a thrilling experience every time. We guaranty fun and entertainment to our numerous customers, as we specialize in creating exciting and exhilarating moments for kids, families, schools, churches etc.
